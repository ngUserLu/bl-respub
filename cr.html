Institution:    University of Kansas
Location:    Golfito, Costa Rica
Period:    June - July 1998
Program:      The Undergraduate Study Abroad program in Golfito, Costa Rica, was in partnership with the Institute of Tropical Studies. The summer-semester length program included two KU Faculty-led upper level-courses, Tropical Ecology and Rainforest Biodiversity. I planned and executed a research project on the distribution of bromeliads. Staying with a local family provided excellent instruction in conversational Spanish.
