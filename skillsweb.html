Over the last two or three years I've become more interested in pure web / HTTP development. With the development of ESRI's web apis and cloud-based GIS travel across the internet has become even more prevalent in GIS. I learned about RESTful web design and Model-View-Controller communication, even Javascript Object Notation in the process of rewriting the City and County of Honolulu's Web ADF ArcObjects modules into a Server Object Extension and then communicating with it from Javascript and Silverlight API-based versions of the SWAPP. After being somewhat hesitant about the cloud in general I've come around to the entire idea after taking advantage of Amazon's first-year free subscription. Cross domain sharing is something else as a developer I've become accustomed to.
